Detailed Description

Implements a general power-law jet profile where properties follow a power-law distribution with angle.

This class provides a model for GRB jets with a power-law decay, characterized by two core angles (one for energy and another for Lorentz factor), isotropic equivalent energy E_iso, initial Lorentz factor Gamma0, and two power-law indices for the energy and Lorentz factor angular profiles.
