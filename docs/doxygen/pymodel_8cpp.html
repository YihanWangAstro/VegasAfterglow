Creates a Gaussian jet model with smooth energy and Lorentz factor profiles.

Generates an ejecta structure representing a Gaussian-structured jet where both the energy density and initial Lorentz factor decrease smoothly from the jet axis following a Gaussian profile. This model provides a more realistic smooth transition compared to the sharp cutoff of top-hat jets and is often used to model structured jets in gamma-ray bursts.

Gamma0Initial bulk Lorentz factor at the jet center
spreadingWhether to include lateral jet spreading during expansion (default: false)
durationDuration of central engine activity [seconds] (default: 1)
magnetarOptional magnetar model for continuous energy injection (default: none)
Returns
